Designer Series

Cemintel Designer Series™ is a modern, innovative and distinctive range of pre-finished walling panels ideal for any façade application and unique in the Australian market. As featured on Channel 9’s The Block and Grand Designs Australia, Designer Series offers:

  • Unique aesthetic appeal, which is lightweight and durable
  • An innovative fixing system, which rapidly reduces installation time
  • A multi layered painting system designed for long lasting performance
  • A high level of texture and depth of colour making it superior to existing brick or fibre cement facades
  • A variety of textures and colours
  • Horizontal and Vertical installation options
  • Internal & external installation options
